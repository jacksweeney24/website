raw unfiltered honey

flows through it like mucus, fluid

filled with trembling bellflowers, paper

windmills made of blue card stock

that rotate slowly in the breeze.

refractions of rainbows and cerulean

glaciers that erode valleys in the grass,

dolphins that porpoise

and sing pure tones in their language,

chandeliers that multiply out in icy fractals,

sparkles turning into stars, they stretch

light years across her belly, body

wearing cute sticky panties

soaked in arizona iced tea.
